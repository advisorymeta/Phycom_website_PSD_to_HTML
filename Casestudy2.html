case study

Rapid startup.

The proper configuration and set up for sales and customer support workflow are critical to a new startup company. Our client had selected Salesforce for this task, however they were extremely busy with product development and other concerns typical in launching a new business.


As an external provider, we were able to swiftly address the challenge. We began by assessing the needs of their sales and customer service teams. By understanding their vision to integrate multiple systems, we arrived at a configuration that satisfied both CRM and sales analytics requirements.


In addition to working with Salesforce, we introduced Confluence and JIRA to chart progress. We also developed a prototype to give team members real-time experience of the new system – as opposed to simply envisioning it from documentation. This allowed us to rapidly make adjustments and refine the details.


The result: they now have an integrated workflow process that fully addresses all aspects of the sales and customer support lifecycle.
